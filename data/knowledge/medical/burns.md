TITLE: Treating Burns

WHEN:
- Skin exposed to fire, hot liquid, steam, or electricity

STEPS:
1. Remove the person from the source of heat
2. Cool the burn with clean running water for 20 minutes
3. Remove tight clothing or jewelry near the burn
4. Cover with clean, non-stick cloth

DO NOT:
- Do not apply butter, oil, toothpaste, or mud
- Do not break blisters
- Do not use ice directly

- Burn is deep
- Burn is on face, hands, feet, or genitals
