TITLE: Headache and Body Pain

COMMON CAUSES:
- Dehydration
- Fever
- Stress
- Lack of sleep

STEPS:
1. Rest in a quiet, dark place
2. Drink clean water
3. Apply cool cloth to forehead
4. Stretch gently if stiff

MEDICINE (IF AVAILABLE):
- Paracetamol may reduce pain

DO NOT:
- Do not take multiple pain medicines together
- Do not use alcohol for pain relief

SOURCE:
WHO Primary Health Care Manual
