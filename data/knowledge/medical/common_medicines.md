TITLE: Common Medicines in Survival Situations

PARACETAMOL:
- Reduces fever and pain
- Safer for stomach

IBUPROFEN:
- Reduces pain and swelling
- Avoid if stomach pain or dehydration

ORS:
- Prevents dehydration
- Life-saving for diarrhea

ANTISEPTICS:
- Clean skin and wounds
- Do not pour deep inside wounds

DO NOT:
- Combine multiple pain medicines
- Use unknown pills
- Guess dosages

SOURCE:
WHO Essential Medicines (Emergency Use)
