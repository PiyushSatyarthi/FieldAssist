TITLE: Snake Bite First Aid

WHEN:
- Bite from a snake or suspected snake bite
- Puncture marks on skin with pain or swelling

IMMEDIATE ACTION:
1. Move the person away from the snake
2. Keep the person calm and still
3. Lay the person down
4. Immobilize the bitten limb using a splint or cloth
5. Keep the bitten area below heart level

DO NOT:
- Do not cut the wound
- Do not suck the venom
- Do not apply ice or chemicals
- Do not use tourniquets
- Do not give alcohol or food

MONITOR FOR:
- Swelling spreading
- Difficulty breathing
- Drowsiness
- Vomiting

SEEK HELP IMMEDIATELY IF:
- Any bite from a snake
- Symptoms appear or worsen

SOURCE:
WHO Snakebite Envenoming Guidelines
