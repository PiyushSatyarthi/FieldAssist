TITLE: Hypothermia

WHEN:
- Cold exposure
- Shivering
- Slurred speech or confusion

STEPS:
1. Move person to warm, dry shelter
2. Remove wet clothing
3. Wrap in dry blankets or clothes
4. Warm slowly using body heat
5. Give warm sweet drinks if conscious

DO NOT:
- Do not rub limbs vigorously
- Do not apply direct heat like fire
- Do not give alcohol

SEEK HELP IF:
- Person is unconscious
- Shivering stops
- Breathing is slow or irregular

SOURCE:
WHO Cold Exposure Guidelines
