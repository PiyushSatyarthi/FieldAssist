TITLE: Managing Diarrhea

WHEN:
- Loose or watery stools multiple times a day

MAIN DANGER:
- Dehydration

STEPS:
1. Drink Oral Rehydration Solution (ORS) if available
2. If ORS unavailable, drink clean water frequently
3. Continue eating light food if possible

MEDICINE:
- ORS is the most important treatment

DO NOT:
- Do not stop fluids
- Do not take anti-diarrheal drugs without guidance

SEEK HELP IF:
- Blood in stool
- Severe weakness
- Diarrhea lasts more than 3 days
- Child becomes drowsy or stops drinking

SOURCE:
WHO Diarrheal Disease Guidelines
