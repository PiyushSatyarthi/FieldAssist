TITLE: Insect Bites and Stings

WHEN:
- Pain, redness, or swelling after insect bite

STEPS:
1. Wash the area with clean water
2. Apply cold compress to reduce swelling
3. Keep area clean and dry

DO NOT:
- Do not scratch the bite
- Do not apply unknown substances

SEEK HELP IF:
- Difficulty breathing
- Swelling of face or throat
- Severe pain or fever

SOURCE:
WHO Vector Control & Health Guide
