TITLE: Heat Exhaustion and Heat Stroke

WHEN:
- Exposure to high heat
- Heavy sweating or no sweating
- Dizziness or confusion

STEPS:
1. Move person to a cool, shaded place
2. Loosen clothing
3. Cool body with wet cloths
4. Fan the person if possible
5. Give small sips of clean water if conscious

DO NOT:
- Do not give alcohol
- Do not give fluids if unconscious

SEEK HELP IMMEDIATELY IF:
- Confusion or unconsciousness
- Seizures
- Body temperature very high

SOURCE:
WHO Heat and Health Action Plan
