TITLE: Fractures and Sprains

WHEN:
- Severe pain after injury
- Swelling or deformity
- Inability to move limb normally

STEPS:
1. Do not move the injured area unnecessarily
2. Immobilize using sticks, boards, or folded cloth
3. Secure splint above and below the injury
4. Apply cold compress if available
5. Keep the person comfortable and warm

DO NOT:
- Do not try to straighten broken bones
- Do not massage the injured area
- Do not allow movement if pain increases

SEEK HELP IF:
- Bone is visible
- Severe pain or swelling
- Loss of feeling in limb

SOURCE:
WHO Emergency Trauma Care Guidelines
