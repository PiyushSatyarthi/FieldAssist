STEP-BY-STEP ACTIONS:

1. RECOGNIZE CONFLICT (DO FIRST):
   - Signs of conflict: Arguments, disagreements, tension, hostility
   - Behavioral signs: Avoidance, aggression, passive-aggression
   - Communication signs: Poor communication, miscommunication, no communication
   - If conflict exists: Recognize immediately
   - Do not deny conflict: Denial prevents resolution
   - Do not ignore conflict: Conflict affects group

2. ASSESS CONFLICT SEVERITY:
   - Minor conflict: Disagreement, tension
   - Moderate conflict: Arguments, disputes
   - Severe conflict: Hostility, threats, violence
   - Do not ignore severity: Severity affects response
   - Do not underestimate: Underestimation causes problems

3. ASSESS CONFLICT CAUSES:
   - Resource competition: Competition for resources
   - Decision disputes: Disagreement about decisions
   - Personality clashes: Personality incompatibility
   - Stress: Stress causing conflict
   - Miscommunication: Miscommunication causing conflict
   - Do not ignore causes: Causes must be addressed
   - Do not assume causes: Verify causes

4. PREVENT ESCALATION:
   - Stop conflict: Stop conflict immediately
   - Separate parties: Separate conflicting parties if needed
   - De-escalate: Use de-escalation techniques
   - Do not let conflict escalate: Escalation causes problems
   - Do not ignore escalation: Escalation is dangerous

5. RESOLVE CONFLICT:
   - Address causes: Address conflict causes
   - Find compromise: Find acceptable compromise
   - Use mediation: Use mediation if available
   - Use discussion: Use discussion to resolve
   - Do not ignore resolution: Resolution is essential
   - Do not force resolution: Forced resolution fails

6. RESTORE HARMONY:
   - Rebuild trust: Rebuild trust between parties
   - Restore communication: Restore group communication
   - Restore cooperation: Restore group cooperation
   - Do not ignore harmony: Harmony is essential
   - Do not rush harmony: Harmony takes time

7. PREVENT RECURRENCE:
   - Address root causes: Address underlying causes
   - Improve communication: Improve group communication
   - Establish protocols: Establish conflict protocols
   - Do not ignore prevention: Prevention prevents recurrence
   - Do not ignore causes: Causes create conflict

8. MAINTAIN GROUP:
   - Keep group together: Maintain group unity
   - Maintain function: Keep group functioning
   - Maintain survival: Keep group surviving
   - Do not let conflict break group: Group is essential
   - Do not ignore group: Group survival depends on unity
